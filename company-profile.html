About VogueVault

VogueVault is a Fashion company that is a pioneer in Women's clothing. VogueVault was founded in 2019 by Miss Rebecca Kirabo and published in 2020. The company main office is located in Entebbe.What started as passion project in Entebbe has now grown into a fast-rising fashion hbrand in Uganda. At VogueVault, we believe that fashion is more than just clothng, it is a voice, an identity, and a form of empowerment. For timeless essentials to statement pieces, our collections combine mordern trends with unique storytelleing, celebrating diversity, culture and creativity in every thread.

Milestones

2020

In 2020, amidst the global COVID-19 lockdown and various business challenges, our Company made a significant strategic shift by launching an online boutique. This move was driven by the urgent need to adapt to a rapidly changing market and ensure continued service to our customers during a time of limited physical interaction. Till date you can always access VogueVault on our online Botique so that you dont miss out on your favourite outfits.

2021

We launched our first fashion show with a runway at Diamond Hotel where our models showcased a couple of fashion inspos.

2024
